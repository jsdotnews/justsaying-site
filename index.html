What this is

Just Saying is an independent civic journalism project. We don't lobby, we don't endorse, and we don't take ads.

We pick things people are talking about—claims, bills, votes—and check them against primary sources. Federal databases, court opinions, the actual bill text. Then we link every source so you can verify it yourself.

We apply the same standard to everyone, regardless of party. If we check a Democrat's claim this week, we'll check a Republican's claim next week.
